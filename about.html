Quick Intro

Hi! My name is Griffin Burke, I'm coming into my Junior year at the University of Illinois studying Computer Science with a Minor in Mathematics (and probably Statistics soon as well).

I know I'm interested in AI/ML, that sort of sphere, but I'm still exploring the different areas within it that I might want to work with. I'm a big fan of building, creating AI-integrated apps to solve problems that I have. But that never seems to scratch the itch that research does, where I'm really getting into the technical aspects of how these models that I'm using actually work, and how we can improve their architecture and deployment.

Within the next year I'm aiming to be a part of that research space and really dig deep, seeing if the type of work is for me, while learning a lot of AI/ML theory. I'd also like to learn how to work with large-scale AI applications. Most of what I've built is local, or simple API calls. I'd like to expand to cloud-based development and deployment of models and apps.
